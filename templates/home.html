About This Project

Diabetes Prediction is a machine-learning web app that estimates diabetes risk using medical inputs such as Glucose, BMI, Blood Pressure, Insulin, and Age.

The platform is designed for quick screening, better awareness, and regular tracking so users can take preventive action early.
